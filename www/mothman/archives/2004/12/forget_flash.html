Forget Flash

ALLADYN Project homepage

The Alladyn project is a cross-platform DHTML library that enables you to create animations and effects, which can replace most functionality of swf files (that being a good thing.)

It is free for non-commercial use, but unfortunately you have to pay to use it commercially:

Cost of Alladyn 1.x is:
Onetime use - 13 €
Full licence - 125 €

fairly steep, but this is good stuff.
