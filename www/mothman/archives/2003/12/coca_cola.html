coca-cola buying up a whole bunch of domains
in an attempt to quash stories of it hiring killers
to kill colombian union leaders.

these domains basically are:
killer{-coke|coke|-cocacola|cocacola}.{net|org|com}
{unions|students}against{coke|cocacola|.{net|org|com}
{coke|cocacola}kills.{net|org|com}
unthinkable[-]undrinkable.{net|org|com}
fight{coke|coca[-]cola}.{net|org|com}

http://www.colombialabor.coca-cola.com/
